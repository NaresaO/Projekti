So who are we exactly?

myTicket is an online platform for purchasing tickets for movies. Our services make it easy to book tickets from several booking websites and make them avaliable in one place, in a single transaction, whether you are in the UK, the USA or Europe. Fees are charged at the point of purchase.

myTicket operates in all EU countries, Iceland, and Norway. The founding team of myTicket was made up of entrepreneurs from London, Oslo and Paris. The company now employs 130 people.

In September 2021, myTicket partnered with Worldline, one of Europe's leading provider of payment services, for myTicket's European movie ticket booking services, allowing the company to expand its presence to 2,500+ Live Nation & AXS corporate clients as well as 20,000+ independent movie theatres and cinema's. myTicket has recieved $1.3m in funding from City Index Ventures in May 2023.
